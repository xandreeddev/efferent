Mediterranean Quinoa Bowl

A vibrant, protein-packed bowl loaded with fresh vegetables, briny olives, tangy feta, and a bright lemon-oregano dressing.

20 min 2 servings Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 oz feta cheese, cubed
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, pitted
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa in 2 cups water with a pinch of salt. Bring to boil, reduce to low, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.

  2. Fluff with fork and let cool slightly.

  3. In a large bowl, combine warm quinoa, chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and parsley.

  4. In a small jar, shake together olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper.

  5. Pour over salad and toss gently.

  6. Top with crumbled feta. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories460 kcal
Protein16g
Carbs52g
Fat22g

Chef's Tip

Toast the quinoa in the dry pot for 2 minutes before adding water — it brings out a nutty flavor that elevates the whole bowl. Rinse quinoa well to remove any bitterness.
