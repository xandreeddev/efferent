Perfect Avocado Toast

Creamy avocado on crisp sourdough, topped with a silky poached egg and bright, punchy seasonings. Breakfast elevated.

10 min 2 servings Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 lemon
  • Red chili flakes
  • Flaky sea salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Microgreens or fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Toast sourdough until golden and crisp.

  2. Halve avocado, remove pit, and scoop into a bowl. Add squeeze of lemon, pinch of salt, and mash with a fork leaving some chunks.

  3. Spread thickly over toast.

  4. Poach eggs in simmering water for 3 minutes.

  5. Top toast with egg, drizzle of olive oil, chili flakes, and sea salt. Garnish with microgreens.

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories340 kcal
Protein14g
Carbs28g
Fat20g

Chef's Tip

The key is a ripe avocado — it should yield gently to pressure. If your avocado is firm, let it sit in a paper bag with a banana for a day to ripen.
